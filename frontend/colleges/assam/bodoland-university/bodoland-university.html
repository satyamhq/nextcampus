About Bodoland University

Bodoland University (BU), established in 2009, is one of Assam's leading state institutions based in Kokrajhar. It is affiliated with Gauhati University / Dibrugarh University and serves thousands of students across Northeast India.

Career & Placements

Graduates from Assam state universities primarily enter APSC (Assam Public Service Commission), Banking, Education (SSA/RMSA), Oil & Gas sector jobs (OIL India, ONGC), and increasingly IT services from Guwahati Infosys/Wipro campuses.

About IIT Madras

Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras) is one of India's premier engineering institutions, established in 1959 as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. Located within a protected forest reserve in Chennai, the sprawling 617-acre green campus is a landmark in itself — home to deer, blackbucks, and bonnet macaques alongside world-class academic infrastructure.

IIT Madras has been ranked #1 in NIRF (Engineering) for multiple consecutive years, and is globally recognized for its research output, innovation ecosystem, and entrepreneurship culture. The institute houses one of India's leading research parks (IITM Research Park) and has incubated 200+ startups through its Centre for Innovation and the Nirmaan Pre-Incubator.

Admission Process

1

Qualify the Entrance Exam

Appear for JEE Advanced (B.Tech), GATE (M.Tech), CAT (MBA), JAM (M.Sc), or institute-specific written test (PhD / MA).

2

JoSAA / CCMT / Institute Counselling

Register for centralized counselling (JoSAA for B.Tech, CCMT for M.Tech) or apply directly to IIT Madras for MBA/PhD programs.

3

Seat Allotment & Acceptance

Accept your allotted seat online and pay the provisional admission fee within the given deadline.

4

Document Verification

Report to IIT Madras for physical document verification, medical check, and hostel allotment.

5

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Complete semester fee payment and confirm final enrollment to begin coursework.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM, min. 75% marks (or top 20 percentile). Must qualify JEE Advanced.
  • M.Tech: B.Tech/B.E. in relevant discipline with valid GATE score.
  • MBA: Any graduation with 60% (50% for SC/ST). Valid CAT score required.
  • M.Sc: B.Sc with relevant major. Valid JAM score required.
  • PhD: PG degree in relevant field. Institute written test and interview.
  • BS Online: 10+2 from any recognized board. Online qualifier exam.

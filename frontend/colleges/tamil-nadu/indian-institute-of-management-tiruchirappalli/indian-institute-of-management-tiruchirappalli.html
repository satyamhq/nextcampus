About Indian Institute of Management Tiruchirappalli

Indian Institute of Management Tiruchirappalli (IIM Trichy) is a premier management institution located in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu. Established in 2011, it holds the status of an Institute of National Importance and is part of the prestigious Indian Institutes of Management (IIM) network, known globally for producing top-tier business leaders, entrepreneurs, and policymakers.

Admission Information

1

Qualify Exam (CAT/GMAT/IPMAT)

The flagship MBA/PGP program requires a highly competitive CAT (Common Admission Test) score, followed by Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI). Executive programs often accept GMAT/GRE scores.

2

Shortlisting & PI

Candidates are shortlisted based on their entrance scores and academic profile for the Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI).

3

Final Selection

Admissions are finalized based on a composite score comprising entrance test, PI, WAT, academic diversity, and work experience.

About Chhattisgarh Engineering College

Chhattisgarh Engineering College (CEC) is a significant educational institution located in Durg, Chhattisgarh. Established in 2008, it has played a vital role in providing quality education and fostering development in central India.

Admission Information

1

Qualify Entrance Exam / Merit

State engineering admissions are primarily through CG PET (Chhattisgarh Pre Engineering Test) and JEE Main through DTE Counseling.

2

Counselling & Allocation

Participate in state (DTE/DME Chhattisgarh) or central counselling processes based on your entrance rank or 12th percentage.

3

Seat Acceptance

Report to the college with original documents and Domicile certificates (if applicable) after seat allocation.

About Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University (CU) is a NAAC A+ accredited private university located in Mohali, Punjab. Established in 2012, CU offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Law, Media, Sciences, and more. The university spans a lush 200+ acre campus on the Chandigarh-Ludhiana Highway and is recognized by UGC and approved by AICTE.

CU is known for its strong placement marketing, industry collaborations, international partnerships, and vibrant student life. The university hosts students from across India and abroad, offering world-class infrastructure including smart classrooms, modern research labs, a central library, and a dedicated placement cell that connects students with top companies globally.

Admission Process

1

Register Online

Visit cuchd.in and create your profile on the CU admissions portal with basic details.

2

Choose Your Program

Select your preferred program and specialization from 150+ available options across all disciplines.

3

Appear for CUCET / Submit Scores

Take the Chandigarh University Common Entrance Test (CUCET) online, or submit valid national exam scores (JEE Main, CAT, MAT, CLAT, GPAT, etc.).

4

Receive Offer Letter

Based on your scores and merit, an admission offer letter will be issued to your registered email.

5

Pay Fees & Confirm Admission

Complete fee payment online and submit documents to confirm your seat.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech: 10+2 with minimum 60% in PCM. Valid JEE Main or CUCET score.
  • MBA: Graduation with 50%. Valid CAT / MAT / CUCET score.
  • BCA / BBA: 10+2 in any stream with 50%. CUCET recommended.
  • Law (BA LLB / BBA LLB): 10+2 with 45%. Valid CLAT or CUCET score.
  • B.Pharm: 10+2 with PCB/PCM and minimum 50%.
  • Hotel Management: 10+2 in any stream from a recognized board.

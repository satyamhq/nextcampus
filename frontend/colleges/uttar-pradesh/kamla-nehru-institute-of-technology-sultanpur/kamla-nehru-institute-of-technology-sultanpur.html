About Kamla Nehru Institute of Technology Sultanpur

Kamla Nehru Institute of Technology Sultanpur (KNIT) forms the backbone of the intricate educational landscape in Sultanpur, Uttar Pradesh. Established in 1976, it channels the enormous talent pool of North India toward state-level bureaucracy, massive IT/manufacturing deployments in the NCR ecosystem, and supreme national technical roles.

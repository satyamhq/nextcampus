About IIT Kanpur

IIT Kanpur (IITK) is one of India's premier engineering institutions, established in 1959 as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. Located in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, IITK is recognized for its research excellence, academic rigor, and strong industry connections.

The institute spans a 1055-acre campus and offers programs across engineering, science, management, and humanities. With a dedicated placement cell and strong alumni network, IITK consistently delivers excellent career outcomes for its graduates.

Admission Process

1

Qualify Entrance Exam

JEE Advanced (B.Tech), GATE (M.Tech), JAM (M.Sc), or institute's written test (PhD).

2

JoSAA / CCMT Counselling

Register for centralized counselling and accept your allotted seat.

3

Pay Provisional Fee

Pay the provisional admission fee online to freeze your seat.

4

Document Verification

Report to the institute campus with original academic certificates.

5

Enrollment & Orientation

Complete enrollment and attend the induction/orientation program.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM, min. 75% (or top 20 percentile). Must qualify JEE Advanced.
  • M.Tech: B.Tech/B.E. in relevant discipline with valid GATE score.
  • M.Sc: B.Sc with relevant subject. Valid JAM score required.
  • PhD: PG degree in relevant field. Institute written test + interview.

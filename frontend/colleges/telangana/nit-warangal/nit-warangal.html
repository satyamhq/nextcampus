About NIT Warangal

NIT Warangal (NITW) is a premier engineering institution, established in 1959 as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. Located in Warangal, Telangana, NITW is recognized for its academic rigor, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections.

The institute spans a 248-acre campus and offers excellent infrastructure, vibrant student life, and a strong alumni network, making it one of the most sought-after NITs in India.

Admission Process

1

Qualify Entrance Exam

JEE Main for B.Tech/B.Arch, GATE for M.Tech, IIT JAM for M.Sc.

2

Centralized Counselling

JoSAA/CSAB (B.Tech), CCMT (M.Tech), or CCMN (M.Sc) based on AIR.

3

Seat Acceptance

Pay provisional fee online to freeze, float, or slide your allocated seat.

4

Physical Verification

Report to the NIT campus with original documents.

5

Orientation

Complete final registration and attend the fresher orientation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM, min. 75% marks (65% for SC/ST). Must rank high in JEE Main.
  • M.Tech: B.Tech/B.E. in relevant discipline with valid GATE score.
  • M.Sc: B.Sc with relevant subject. Valid IIT JAM score required.
  • PhD: Master's in relevant field. Institute written test + interview.

About St Albert's College Ernakulam

St Albert's College Ernakulam (SAC), established in 1959, is one of Kerala's premier private aided institutions based in Ernakulam. It is affiliated with or operates under the purview of the University of Kerala / MG University / Calicut University / CUSAT ecosystem, providing robust academic and career outcomes.
